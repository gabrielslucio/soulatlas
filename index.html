The Firelance

It is a grey morning on the steps of the Hulacris Plane, as a convoy of soldiers return to the village of Acrenod. Their bodies and faces weary, their numbers reduced, for they are survivors from the campaign in the Murr heartlands. After dismounting from their horses, one of the soldiers, Tren, hands a small, mysterious box to the blacksmith Norm. Unbeknownst to him, the box's contents could forever alter not just his destiny but the very fabric of their world.

"The Firelance," is a riveting journey set in Soul Atlas during the dangerous times of the Murr Invasion. This 9-chapter short story, releasing on March 10th, 2024, chronicles the transformation of Norm, a humble blacksmith, into one of the most influential figures of the Age of Imperials. Amidst the flames of war, Norm's destiny is forged, altering the course of history. Embark on this epic adventure with "The Firelance," available on YouTube and the free Soul Atlas App. Join us on March 10th to discover a story where a single invention could change everything.
